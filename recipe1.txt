Sweet Potato Spice Smoothie Bowl

Start your day off with a nourishing smoothie bowl featuring warm flavors of pumpkin spice and sweet potato.

Ingredients:
1 cup almond milk
¾ cup sweet potato puree
¼ cup Nature’s Path Old Fashioned Oats
2 tbsp pure maple syrup
½ tsp pure vanilla extract
½ tsp ground cinnamon
¼ tsp ground ginger
⅛ tsp ground nutmeg
Pinch of cloves
Sprinkle of Nature’s Path Coconut Chia Granola (optional).

Directions:
In a blender, combine all ingredients until smooth. For a smoothie to-go, pour into a mason jar. For a smoothie breakfast bowl, pour into a bowl and top with Nature’s Path Coconut Chia Granola.
